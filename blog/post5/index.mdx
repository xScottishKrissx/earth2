Each of the arks are designed to transfer human in as smooth and relaxed manner as possible.

On arrival at your designated launch platform, you will be directed to your very own hibernation chamber where you will be made as comfortable as possible.

After all it's a long journey and centuries old bed sores might ruin the mood upon arrival.
